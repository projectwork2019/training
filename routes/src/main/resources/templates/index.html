Online Coordination Games

Purpose of the experiments

We study pure coordination games, where in every outcome all players have identical payoffs, `win' or `lose'. The players are assumed to make their choices based only on common knowledge of rationality and pure reasoning, without any preplay communication or conventions. With these experiments we want to collect empirical data on the principles guiding purely rational human reasoning.
